Japanese Curry

Japan's weird flex on Indians that turned out all right

India invented the curry. The British brought it to Japan. And the Japanese decided to make their own version.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice

Instructions

  • Saute the onions
  • Gently lay the chicken in the pan, as if you're tucking in a child

  • Make some rice with a rice cooker
