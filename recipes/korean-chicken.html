Korean Fried Chicken

The real KFC

Possibly my favorite fried chicken. I don't actually fry it though, that's too much of a hassle. This is where my airfryer comes in handy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Gochujang Sauce

Recipe instructions

  1. Coat the chicken with seasonings and corn starch
  2. Put it in the airfryer
  3. Make the sauce while you wait
  4. Mix the sauce with the chicken
